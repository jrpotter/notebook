## Overview

A **parabola** is the set of points whose distance to a fixed point, the **focus**, equals the distance to a fixed line, the **directrix**. The point where this distance is minimal is called the **vertex**. The distance from vertex to the focus (or directrix) is the **focal length** of the parabola.

![[locus-parabola.png]]

It can also be defined as the conic section formed by orienting the cutting plane parallel to the slant of the double cone.

![[conic-parabola.png]]
