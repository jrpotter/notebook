## Overview

Hangul (한글) refers to the Korean alphabet. Syllables consist of at least one consonant and one vowel. Hangul is written left to right, top to bottom.

## Vowels

There are 10 basic vowels. In dictionary order, they are:

* 아
* 야
* 어
* 여
* 오
* 요
* 우
* 유
* 으
* 이
