## Overview

**Wi-Fi** (Wireless-Fidelity) refers to the IEEE 802.11 set of standards.

%%ANKI
Basic
Why is Wi-Fi named the way it is?
Back: It's short for **wi**reless-**fi**delity.
Reference: Fall, Kevin R, and W Richard Stevens. “TCP/IP Illustrated, Volume 1: The Protocols,” n.d.
